Why is credit score important?

Your credit score affects:

  • Your ability to get new loans or mortgages;
  • Your interest rate: The higher your credit score, the lower your interest rate;
  • Financial flexibility in an emergency.

A lower credit score can shut off your access to financial tools, making even emergency loans difficult and expensive. It's a vicious cycle that's hard to get out of without the right approach.
